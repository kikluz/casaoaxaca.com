Our mission is to provide customers with the finest Mexican products for gifts, home, and garden.

While everything in the store comes from various regions throughout Mexico, the majority of our merchandise comes from the State of Oaxaca. Guillermo ("Memo") Robles (the owner) makes frequent trips to various regions of Mexico (such as Mexico, Puebla, Chiapas, Oaxaca and Guerrero) with his family to individually select each piece he brings back to the store.

All purchasing trips are made with the customers' wants and tastes in mind.
